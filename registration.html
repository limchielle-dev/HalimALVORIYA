How to Join ALVORIYA

Joining ALVORIYA is a straightforward process. We look for passionate students who are ready to contribute and learn. Here is what we expect from our members:

  • Application: Complete the registration form below with your valid student details.
  • Academic Standing: Open to all active students with a passion for technology and leadership.
  • Engagement: Enthusiastic participation in at least two club activities per month.
  • Interview: A brief casual meet-and-greet with our board members to get to know you better.
